Getting Started

CloudCoach requires an Anthropic API key to function. You can get one at console.anthropic.com.

Enter your API key in the macOS app's Settings (Cmd+,). The iOS app connects to the Mac app and does not need its own key.

Connecting iOS to Mac

Both devices should be on the same network. The macOS app displays a QR code — scan it with the iOS app to pair. Once paired, the connection is remembered and reconnects automatically.

FAQ

How much does the AI cost to use?

You pay Anthropic directly for API usage. Typical coaching conversations cost $0.01-0.10 per interaction depending on complexity. The macOS app shows your running cost in the dashboard.

Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored locally on your Mac in a DuckDB database inside the app's container. Nothing is sent to our servers.
